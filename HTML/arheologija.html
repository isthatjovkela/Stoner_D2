If you too are interested in Archaeology like Stoner D2, here is an introduction to Archaeology:

Archaeology is the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation and analysis of material remains, such as artifacts, architecture, ecofacts, and cultural landscapes. It is a multidisciplinary field that combines elements of anthropology, history, geography, geology, and other disciplines to understand past human cultures and societies.The primary goal of archaeology is to reconstruct and interpret the lifeways, behaviors, and cultural practices of past peoples. Archaeologists use a variety of methods and techniques to investigate archaeological sites, including excavation, surveying, laboratory analysis, and interpretation of data.
